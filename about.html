About Hotel Regis

Embodying the 110-year-old legacy of John Jacob Astor IV, our 5-star hotel in South Mumbai made its debut as the first St. Regis hotel in India. Every element of the hotel is infused with timeless elegance, from the luxurious rooms and suites to our expansive ballrooms, banquet halls, and outdoor venues, highlighted by our award-winning specialty restaurants with breathtaking panoramic views of the cityscape.

The lavishly designed one-bedroom apartments make our hotel the perfect choice for families or those seeking extra room to relax. Nestled within the High Street Phoenix in Lower Parel, our hotel is just steps away from premium shopping & entertainment and major commercial business districts. Immerse yourself in luxury hotel amenities, including the treasured St. Regis Butler Service, as you explore the finest offerings of Mumbai.
